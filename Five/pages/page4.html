Me Or Us

Young Thug • Beautiful Thugger Girls

Why This Song

This is one of the slowest tracks on the album Young Thug released over the summer. Although the album in a whole was a switch-up from his regular hard-hitting, spastic rap (which don't get me wrong, I love), it was nice to hear a softer side of Thugger. "Me Or Us" revolves around his current relationship and I wasn't able to truly appreciate the lyrics until recently. If this was done by any other artist I wouldn't think it was anything more than pretty, but Young Thug gets on proves he can rap over anything and for that reason, it comes in as one of my favorites.
