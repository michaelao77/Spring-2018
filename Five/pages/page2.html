Feels Like We Only Go Backwards - Live

Tame Impala • Live Versions

Why This Song

Although I had been listening to the album version of this song, I started listening to the live version in preparation for seeing them in concert. Not only does the roar of the crowd make it feel like you're there, Kevin Parker changes his vocals to hits I didn't even know he was capable of. This song is authentic and desperate and passionate and I love it. Voice cracks and all, I feel like this song could break the stats bar.
