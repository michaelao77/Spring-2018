Call It Fate, Call It Karma

The Strokes • Comedown Machine

Why This Song

This is the current track I can't stop hitting replay for. I am honestly not a huge fan of the verses, but the chorus makes it worth the repeat. I don't know how to describe it other than perfect music. No matter where I am, this song has the ability to put me in a trance. As a testiment to the music, I didn't even know what they were until writing them here. Even the vocals don't really do anything for me and air a bit on the creepy side. Nonetheless, the music and replayabilty of the chorus is what qualitfies it as a favorite.
