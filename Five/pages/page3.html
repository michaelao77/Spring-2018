Jobless Monday

Mitski • Bury Me At Makeout Creek

Why This Song

This is one of the most unplayed songs by my favorite artist, Mitski. I'm drawn to it because of the verse - the way the music builds up and how she uses her voice to create a rise and fall in the melody. The song climaxes to an electronic break and then she continues her moody chant until the song desecalates gently. It's easy to replay for that reason.
