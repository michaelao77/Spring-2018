Why This Song

I discovered this song a couple months ago after getting really into The Beatles and just thought it was perfect. It's calm, subtle and sweet. It also happens to be Paul McCartney’s favourite song of his own composition and was ranked #4 on Mojo Magazine's "Best Songs Ever Written" (© Genius).
